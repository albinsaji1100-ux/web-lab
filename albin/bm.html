BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke AG)

BMW is a German multinational company that designs and manufactures luxury vehicles and motorcycles.
The name BMW stands for "Bayerische Motoren Werke", which translates to "Bavarian Motor Works" in English.
Brands Owned: BMW MINI Rolls-Royce Motor Cars

Sedans & Coupes: 3 Series – Compact executive sedan, one of BMW's most popular lines. 5 Series – Mid-size luxury sedan. 7 Series – Full-size luxury sedan, flagship model with high-end tech. 4 Series, 6 Series, 8 Series – Coupes and Gran Coupes with sporty and elegant styling.
